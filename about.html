About Us

Our goal at The Grand Avenue Cafe is to provide a comfy atmosphere with great food and drinks. Our menu features a book theme; most of the food items are named after classic book titles. This idea was crafted by the original owners: Thomas Knowlton and his kids. Now, almost 20 years later, the current owner, Debra Retallick, maintains this book theme. The Grand Avenue Cafe, located in Eau Claire, WI, offers guests a comfy and cozy atmosphere to enjoy their meal.
